Tax-Free Childcare

For every £8 you pay in, the government will add £2, up to a maximum of £2,000 per child per year (or £4,000 if your child is disabled).

  • check_circle Available for children up to age 11
  • check_circle Direct payment via government portal

15 & 30 Free Hours

Eligible working parents of 2, 3 and 4-year-olds can access up to 30 hours of free childcare per week during term time (38 weeks a year).
